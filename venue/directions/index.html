Directions to the venue

From Dallas (or North Austin) via Interstate 35

Driving South on Interstate 35 into Austin, take the #239/240A St. John’s/US183 exit and turn right at the light to go north on Hwy 183. After 1/2 mile, take the on-ramp from the left lane to enter the highway. Once you are on Hwy 183 North, take the Burnet Road exit and turn left at the light under the overpass. Continue for 1.5 miles and pass West Anderson Lane. Turn right at the entrance between Sonic and Chase Bank. Go to the stop sign and continue straight ahead and Norris Conference Centers will be on your right.

From Houston (or Elgin, Manor or East Austin) via Hwy 290

Upon entering Austin, take the US 183/Lampasas Exit. Keep right at the corner to go north on Hwy 183. Continue for 4 miles and take the Burnet Road exit. Turn left at the light under the overpass. Continue for about 1.5 miles and pass West Anderson Lane. Turn right at the entrance between Sonic and Chase Bank. Go to the stop sign and continue straight ahead and Norris Conference Centers will be on your right.

Austin Bergstrom International Airport (AUS) or from Houston via Hwy 71

Coming in from Houston, pass the Austin airport then take the US 183 North ramp. Or if you are coming from the main terminal of the airport, just as you leave the main road of ABIA turn left (east) towards Austin and take the US 183 North ramp. After nearly 2 miles, the road will fork between Airport Blvd. And US 183 North - stay right and continue on US 183 North. Travel for 6 miles and take the Burnet Road Exit. Turn left at the light under the overpass. Continue for about 1.5 miles and pass West Anderson Lane. Turn right at the entrance between Sonic and Chase Bank. Go to the stop sign and continue straight ahead and Norris Conference Centers will be on your right.

From San Antonio (or San Marcos or South Austin) via Interstate 35

Driving northbound on Interstate 35, take the exit ramp #240B US 183 North/Research Blvd. Merge onto US Hwy 183 and travel north. Take the Burnet Road Exit and turn left at the light under the overpass. Continue for about 1.5 miles and pass West Anderson Lane. Turn right at the entrance between Sonic and Chase Bank. Go to the stop sign and continue straight ahead and Norris Conference Centers will be on your right.

From Southwest Austin or the Texas Hill Country

Take Mopac (Loop 1) North to the Spicewood Springs/Anderson Lane exit. Turn right at the light to go east on Anderson Lane. Travel 1 mile and turn right on Northcross Drive. Turn left at the first stop sign. Drive to the next stop sign and turn right. Drive counter-clockwise around the building towards the right and the Norris Conference Centers will be on your left just past the mall entrance.
