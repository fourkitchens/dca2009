Birds of a Feather sessions

Unlike the regular sessions, Birds of a Feather (BoF) are more informal or can even take the form of a group discussion. The idea is that a group of people gather to discuss a shared topic of interest or have a working session on a particular issue. You can bring a pre-made slide deck if you like, but it’d be a lot cooler if you didn’t.
